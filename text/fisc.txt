Appliitions Made to the Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Court During Calendar
Year 12 (section 107r of the Act, 50 U.S.C. 1807)

During calendar year 2012, the Government made 1,856 applications to the Foreign
Intelligence Surveillance Court (the for authority to conduct electronic surveillance
andfor physical searches for foreign intelligence purposes. The 1,856 applications include
applications made solely for electronic surveillance, applications made solely for physihal search,
and combined applications requesting authority for electronic surveillance and physical Search.
Of these, 1,789 applications included requests for authority to conduct electronic surveillance.

 

Of these 1,789 applications, one was withdrawn by the Government. The  did not
deny any applications in whole or in part.
