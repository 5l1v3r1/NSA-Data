(S//NF) The TCB Jamboree is a conference hosted on an annual basis by the Trusted Computing Base
(TCB) Program within CIA's Emerging Technologies Branch within IOC. TCB Jamboree brings
together CIA analysts and all of the various contractors performing research for the CIA TCB program. In
past years CIA has also invited NS A to participate through the NS A TCB Program (the counterpart to the
CIA program).

(U//FOUO) The first Jamboree was held in 2006. Attendance is open only to US personnel.
