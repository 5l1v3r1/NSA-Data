(S//SI//REL TO USA, JOR) Subject: NSA Intelligence Relationship with Jordan

(S//SI//REL TO USA, JOR) NSA’s partnership with EWD is a well established, long-
standing and trusted relationship dating back to the early 1980’s.

(S//SI//REL TO USA, JOR) EWD provides high-interest, unique collection on targets of
mutual interest, such as the Palestinian Security Forces; EWD is the sole contributor to
a large body of NSA’s reporting on this target.
